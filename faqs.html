Registration Questions

How do I register to vote? +
To register, click the "Register to Vote" button on the homepage. You'll need your official student ID and a valid email address. The system will verify your student ID with the university database before allowing registration.
What if my student ID is not recognized? +
If your student ID isn't recognized, please ensure you're entering it correctly. The system checks against the official university database. If problems persist, contact the election administration or your student services office.
Can I change my registration information? +
Once registered, you can update your email and full name by logging into your account. However, your student ID cannot be changed as it's used for verification purposes.
What are the password requirements? +
Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number. This ensures your account remains secure.
Is there a registration deadline? +
Registration typically closes 24 hours before voting begins. Check the election timeline on the homepage for specific dates and deadlines.

Voting Process

How do I cast my vote? +
After logging in during the voting period, you'll see all available positions and candidates. Select one candidate for each position, review your choices, and click "Submit My Vote". Your vote is final and cannot be changed.
Can I vote more than once? +
No, each registered student can only vote once. The system prevents multiple voting attempts and tracks this for security purposes while maintaining ballot anonymity.
What are the voting hours? +
Voting is typically open from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM on election day. The exact times are displayed on the main page and in your student dashboard.
Is my vote anonymous? +
Yes, your vote is completely anonymous. While the system tracks that you voted (to prevent multiple voting), it cannot link your specific vote choices back to your identity.
Can I vote from my phone? +
Yes! The voting system is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and computers. You can vote from any device with internet access.
What if I make a mistake before submitting? +
You can change your selections before clicking "Submit My Vote." The system shows a summary of your choices for review. However, once submitted, votes cannot be changed.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported? +
UCES works with all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, please use the latest version of your preferred browser.
I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do? +
First, ensure you're using the correct student ID and password. If you've forgotten your password, try refreshing the page. If problems persist, your account may be temporarily locked due to multiple failed attempts.
The page won't load properly. What's wrong? +
Try refreshing your browser, clearing your cache, or using a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues continue, the system may be experiencing high traffic.
Is the voting system secure? +
Yes, UCES uses enterprise-level security including encrypted connections, secure authentication, rate limiting, and comprehensive audit logging to ensure election integrity.

General Questions

When will results be announced? +
Results are typically available immediately after voting closes. You can view live results on the Results page, which updates automatically as votes are counted.
Who can I contact for help? +
For technical issues, contact IT support. For election-related questions, reach out to the Student Council or Election Committee. Contact information is available in the Contact section below.
Can I run for multiple positions? +
Typically, students can only run for one position per election. Check your university's election rules for specific guidelines about candidacy requirements and restrictions.
